
Final Rainbow Poem Package — ready for GitHub Pages

Files to upload to repo root:
- index.html
- love.html
- style.css
- script.js
- background.jpg  (your background image)
- music.mp3       (your audio file)
- lyrics.lrc      (LRC lyrics file)
- flower.png      (optional)

Behavior:
- Open index.html, click "进入表白" to go to love.html.
- Poem displays with large, rounded, fixed rainbow gradient text, typing line-by-line without layout jumps.
- Click play to play music; lyrics.lrc will sync and center the active line in the lyrics box.
